Plant Tips

Explore our top categories for plant lovers — whether you're a beginner, live in low light, or just forget to water, there's something here for everyone.

Easy to Maintain Plants for Beginners

Starting with houseplants can be intimidating, but not all plants require a green thumb. For beginners, there are plenty of hardy, low-maintenance options that thrive with minimal care.

Flowers That Last Long

Fresh flowers can brighten any space, but some blooms fade faster than others. Choose flowers with an extended lifespan to keep your home vibrant and lively longer.

Plants for Apartments with Low Sunlight

Not all homes have bright windows. Luckily, some plants thrive in low-light conditions, perfect for apartments, offices, or dim corners.

Plants That Don’t Need To Be Watered Often

For busy or forgetful plant lovers, drought-tolerant plants are lifesavers. These hardy greens can go weeks without watering and still look fabulous.
